Green Token System

Earn Rewards for Sustainable Practices

Our Green Token system incentivizes farmers to adopt sustainable agricultural practices by rewarding them with digital tokens that can be redeemed for various benefits.

Environmental Impact

Tokens are awarded based on the reduction of food waste and chemical usage in your farming practices.

Economic Benefits

Redeem tokens for discounts on agricultural inputs, premium market access, or even cash rewards.

Market Advantage

Green Token holders receive priority access to premium markets that value sustainable produce.

How to Generate Green Tokens

1

Register Your Farm

Create an account and register your farming details in our system to start tracking your sustainable practices.

2

Install Kand Sanrakshan

Set up our smart storage system to automatically monitor and reduce your post-harvest losses.

3

Track Your Impact

Our system automatically calculates your waste reduction and environmental impact metrics.

4

Earn Tokens

Receive Green Tokens based on your verified reduction in food waste and chemical usage.

5

Redeem Rewards

Use your tokens to access premium markets, get discounts on inputs, or exchange for cash.

6

Grow Your Business

Leverage your sustainable credentials to access better financing and expand your operations.

Contributing to UN Sustainable Development Goals

Kand Sanrakshan directly contributes to 7 UN Sustainable Development Goals through our innovative approach to reducing food waste and promoting sustainable agriculture.

SDG 2: Zero Hunger

Reducing post-harvest losses increases food availability, contributing to food security.

SDG 8: Decent Work & Economic Growth

Creating new opportunities in sustainable agriculture and reducing economic losses for farmers.

SDG 9: Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure

Developing innovative agricultural technologies and building resilient infrastructure.

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities & Communities

Reducing food waste in supply chains contributes to more sustainable consumption patterns.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption & Production

Promoting sustainable food systems and reducing waste throughout the supply chain.

SDG 13: Climate Action

Reducing methane emissions from decomposing food waste and promoting climate-resilient agriculture.

SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals

Collaborating with farmers, governments, and organizations to achieve sustainable development.

Implementation Timeline

Phase 1: Prototype Development (Completed)

Developed and tested the core technology including ethylene sensors, misting system, and IoT connectivity.

Phase 2: Pilot Testing (Current)

Deploying systems with select farmers to validate effectiveness and gather feedback for improvements.

Phase 3: Green Token Integration (Q3 2025)

Implementing the Green Token reward system and connecting with partner organizations.

Phase 4: Scale Up (Q1 2026)

Expanding to more regions and developing additional features based on user feedback.

Phase 5: Full Deployment (Q4 2026)

Nationwide rollout with complete Green Token ecosystem and partnerships with major agricultural organizations.
