The Onion Storage Problem

India's Onion Crisis

India faces significant post-harvest losses in onions due to inadequate storage facilities, leading to:

  • 30-40% onion loss during storage due to sprouting & rotting
  • Current solutions like silica gel and alumina beads are costly & unsafe
  • Cold storage has high energy consumption, unaffordable for small farmers
  • Seasonal price fluctuations due to supply inconsistencies

Our Smart Solution

Smart Ethylene Monitoring

Real-time sensing with ME3-C₂H₄ sensor connected to Arduino UNO and NodeMCU to prevent premature sprouting and spoilage.

Dynamic Temperature Control

PWM-controlled Peltier module adjusts cooling intensity for optimal storage conditions.

Ethylene Absorption

Ultrasonic mist maker creates fog from K₂MnO₄ solution that oxidizes ethylene quickly.

IoT Integration

MQTT protocol for real-time monitoring & alerts accessible to farmers remotely.

How It Works

Ethylene Detection

Sensors continuously monitor ethylene levels in the storage environment.

Smart Response

When ethylene exceeds threshold, the system activates the misting mechanism.

Ethylene Neutralization

K₂MnO₄ mist oxidizes ethylene gas, preventing sprouting and rot.

Temperature Control

Peltier modules maintain optimal storage temperature with minimal energy use.
